Cerebral Palsy Awareness

Overview

Cerebral Palsy is a group of conditions that appear in infancy or preschool and can affect posture and movement.

It is usually caused by brain damage before birth.

The symptoms and effects can include overly stiff or floppy muscles, which impact movements such as walking and causes a lack of muscle coordination and balance. Tremors and trouble speaking and eating can also occur.

How You can Help

You can help people with cerebral palsy by supporting the Cerebral Palsy Foundation. The Cerebral Palsy Foundation helps bring awareness and research for cerebral palsy. You can help support by donating to them in order to improve research and the quality of life for people with cerebral palsy.
